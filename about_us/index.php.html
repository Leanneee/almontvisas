About Us

Almont Visas is a part of Almont Group. We are experts in visa and passport services in Central London and handle numerous business and tourist visa applications per year for our clients travelling to more than 100 countries.

Whether you are travelling for business purposes or visiting your friends and family, heading off to fulfill your dreams of adventures overseas or performing in a concert or gig abroad - our professional team will make getting the visa you need fast and stress-free.

As visa specialists with years of experience in the industry Almont Visas ensures every detail of your application is presented in the correct way with the appropriate embassy. Visa applications are complicated and can be very overwhelming to many people, we make sure everything is arranged properly for you and you don't have to worry about anything.

Why choose us?
We have an online visa status engine which allows you to track your visa application 24/7.
We are always there to answer your queries.
We have years of experience in visa processing and strong relationships with embassies.
Your company can save time and money by delegating your visa arrangements to us.
We are there for you if your colleagues have to travel internationally on regular basis.
We always make sure that we get the right visa to suit your travel arrangements.
